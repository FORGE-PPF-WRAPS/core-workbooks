## Exclusions

The following are **not** covered under this workmanship warranty:

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Pre-existing paint, glass, or body damage documented before service</li>
<li>Damage from accidents, collisions, vandalism, or environmental events</li>
<li>Improper care, abrasive cleaners, or failure to follow aftercare instructions</li>
<li>Modifications by third parties after install</li>
<li>Normal wear, fading, or cosmetic changes per manufacturer specifications</li>
<li>Commercial or fleet use beyond stated limits (if applicable)</li>
<li>Removal and reinstallation by parties other than the issuing shop</li>
</ul>

## Customer responsibilities

To maintain warranty coverage, the customer agrees to:

1. Follow all written and verbal aftercare instructions provided at delivery
2. Notify the shop promptly of any defect within the warranty period
3. Allow the shop reasonable opportunity to inspect and remedy covered claims
4. Not attempt self-repair of warranted installations

---

## Claim procedure

1. Contact the shop within the warranty period with certificate # and description of issue
2. Provide photos if requested
3. Schedule inspection at shop location (or as mutually agreed)
4. Covered claims will be remedied at no charge for labor; material costs per manufacturer policy
