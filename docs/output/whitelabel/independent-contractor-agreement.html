Company and Contractor are each a "Party" and collectively the "Parties."

1. Engagement

Company engages Contractor to perform the services described in Exhibit A (Scope of Services). Contractor accepts such engagement and agrees to perform services as an independent contractor, not as an employee, partner, or agent of Company.

2. Independent contractor status

Contractor represents and agrees that:

  • Contractor is free to control the manner and means of performing services, subject to Company quality standards
  • Contractor is responsible for all taxes, withholdings, and insurance applicable to Contractor
  • Contractor will not receive employee benefits from Company
  • Contractor may perform services for other clients, provided no conflict with Section 8 (Confidentiality)
  • Contractor will supply own tools unless otherwise agreed in writing

4. Term & termination
Start date
End date ☐ Ongoing until terminated

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with _____ days written notice. Company may terminate immediately for material breach, safety violation, or conduct harmful to Company reputation. Upon termination, Contractor shall return all Company property and complete work in progress per Company direction.

5. Quality standards & compliance

Contractor shall perform all services in a professional manner consistent with industry standards for automotive restyling, paint protection film, window tint, and vinyl wrap installation. Contractor shall comply with applicable laws, shop safety policies, and manufacturer installation guidelines when using Company-supplied materials.

6. Insurance

Contractor shall maintain, at Contractor's expense:

  • General liability insurance: minimum $________ per occurrence
  • Automotive / garagekeepers coverage (if applicable)
  • Workers compensation or equivalent (if required by law)

Certificate of insurance shall be provided upon request.

7. Confidentiality

Contractor shall not disclose Company confidential information, including customer data, pricing, processes, training materials, and business strategies, during or after this Agreement.

8. Non-solicitation (optional — customize per jurisdiction)

During the term and for _____ months after termination, Contractor shall not solicit Company's employees or active customers for competing services without Company written consent.

9. Intellectual property

Work product created specifically for Company in performance of services shall be owned by Company upon full payment, unless otherwise agreed in a separate written IP agreement.

10. Indemnification

Contractor shall indemnify and hold harmless Company from claims arising from Contractor's negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of this Agreement, except to the extent caused by Company's negligence.

11. Governing law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE], without regard to conflict-of-law principles. Disputes shall be resolved in courts located in [COUNTY, STATE].

12. Entire agreement

This Agreement, including exhibits, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes prior discussions. Amendments must be in writing signed by both Parties.
