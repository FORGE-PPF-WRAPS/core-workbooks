Assignor has created or will create certain work product in connection with services for Company. This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") clarifies ownership.

1. Assigned work product

Assignor assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in the following ("Assigned IP"):

  • Inventions, processes, and techniques developed for Company
  • Training content, SOPs, manuals, and curricula created for Company
  • Marketing copy, designs, and graphics commissioned by Company
  • Software, templates, spreadsheets, and digital tools built for Company
  • Photography and video of Company work product (if created by Assignor on Company time/equipment)
  • Improvements to Company existing IP

2. Scope of assignment

Assignment includes all copyrights, trademarks (if applicable), trade secrets, patent rights (if registrable), and moral rights to the extent waivable under applicable law.

Assignment is worldwide, perpetual, and irrevocable upon creation, or upon payment if specified in Section 4.
