3. Partner obligations

Partner agrees to:

  1. Represent Company services accurately and professionally
  2. Not make warranties or promises on Company's behalf
  3. Not use Company's trademarks except as authorized in writing
  4. Comply with applicable advertising and disclosure laws
  5. Not engage in spam, misleading claims, or unethical referral practices

4. Company obligations

7. No exclusivity

This Agreement does not create an exclusive relationship. Partner may refer customers to other providers. Company may engage other referral partners and marketing channels.
