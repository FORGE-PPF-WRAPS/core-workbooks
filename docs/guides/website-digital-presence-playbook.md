# Website & Digital Presence Playbook

**Launch and operate your shop's online presence**

---

## Purpose

Step-by-step guide for deploying the website, lead capture, document portal, and digital customer journey that supports the Skill Forge turnkey business system.

---

## Required site structure

| Page | URL slug | Primary goal |
|------|----------|--------------|
| Home | `/` | Trust, services overview, quote CTA |
| PPF | `/services/ppf` | Education, packages, gallery, book |
| Window Tint | `/services/window-tint` | VLT education, legal compliance, book |

| Contact | `/contact` | Form, phone, map, hours |

---

## Homepage essentials

<div class="procedure">

### Above the fold
1. Hero: primary service photo + headline + subhead
2. Primary CTA: **Get a Quote** or **Book Consultation**
3. Phone number visible on mobile

### Trust block
1. Google review score and count
2. Years in business / vehicles protected
3. Manufacturer certifications or film brands used

### Services grid
1. One card per service line with photo and 2-line description
2. Each card links to dedicated service page

### Social proof
1. 3–6 recent project photos
2. Link to full gallery

</div>

## Lead capture

| Channel | Tool | Integration |
|---------|------|-------------|
| Contact form | Website form → CRM | Auto-tag lead source |
| Phone | Tracked number | Log in CRM within 1 hour |
| Text / DM | Business number | Same scripts as CS manual |
| Walk-in | Paper or tablet intake | Vehicle inspection form |

**Required form fields:** Name, phone, email, vehicle YMM, service interest, how they heard about you.

---

## SEO basics

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Title tag per page: `[Service] in [City] | [Shop Name]`</li>
<li>Meta description: 150–160 characters with service + location + CTA</li>
<li>Google Business Profile — complete, photos updated weekly</li>
<li>Schema markup: LocalBusiness + Service</li>
<li>Alt text on all gallery images: year, make, service, result</li>
</ul>

---

## Document library portal

Host or link to **`library/index.html`** — single page listing all branded PDFs:

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Operations manuals (staff login or public summary pages)</li>
<li>Customer aftercare and warranty policy</li>
<li>Training course descriptions (not full workbooks — enrollment required)</li>
</ul>

## Launch checklist

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Domain and SSL configured</li>
<li>Mobile responsive test on iOS and Android</li>
<li>Contact form delivers to CRM/email</li>
<li>Analytics installed (GA4 or equivalent)</li>
<li>Google Business Profile linked</li>
<li>All service pages live with at least 3 photos each</li>
<li>Warranty and aftercare PDFs linked from Resources page</li>
</ul>
