# PPF Package Pricing Guide

**Editable front-desk pricing matrix — adjust for your market**

---

## Package tiers

| Package | Coverage | Typical hours | Suggested retail | Notes |
|---------|----------|---------------|------------------|-------|
| **Partial Front** | Partial hood, partial fenders, mirrors | 4–6 | $________ | Entry tier |
| **Full Front** | Full hood, full fenders, bumper, mirrors | 8–12 | $________ | Most popular |
| **Track Pack** | Full front + rocker panels | 12–16 | $________ | High-wear add-on |
| **Full Body** | All painted panels | 24–40+ | $________ | Quote per vehicle |
| **High-Impact Add-ons** | Headlights, door cups, door edges, rear splash | 1–3 each | $________ each | Upsell at intake |

---

## Vehicle multipliers

| Category | Multiplier | Examples |
|----------|------------|----------|
| Compact sedan | 1.0× | Civic, Corolla |
| Mid-size / SUV | 1.15–1.25× | Camry, RAV4 |
| Full-size truck | 1.2–1.35× | F-150, Silverado |
| Exotic / complex | 1.5–2.0× | Low front, complex curves |
| Fleet (5+ vehicles) | 0.85–0.92× | Volume discount |

---

## Margin targets

| Line item | Target gross margin |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Labor | 55–65% |
| Material (film) | Account for 10–15% waste factor |
| Combined job | 50–60% minimum |

---

## Quote presentation

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Present 2–3 tiers — never single price only</li>
<li>Itemize coverage zones on estimate (`service-estimate-quote`)</li>
<li>Include warranty tier and registration timeline</li>
<li>Deposit: 50% standard for jobs over $1,500</li>
</ul>
