# PPF Surface Preparation SOP

**Decontamination sequence before pattern touch or bulk install**

---

## Purpose

Ensure paint is chemically clean and mechanically smooth before PPF adhesive contact.

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Step 1 — Wash
1. Foam pre-rinse, two-bucket hand wash, top to bottom
2. Dry with clean microfiber — no air-dry on paint

### Step 2 — Decontaminate
1. Clay bar with lubricant on all install zones
2. Iron fallout remover — dwell per product, rinse thoroughly
3. Tar remover on lower panels if needed

### Step 3 — Final wipe
1. IPA wipe (70% isopropyl) on every panel receiving film
2. One direction, clean microfiber per 2 panels
3. Final inspection under cross-light — no contamination visible

### Step 4 — Lock environment
1. Close bay doors; minimize traffic until install complete
2. Do not apply tire dressing or quick detailer after IPA wipe

</div>

## Checkpoint

Paint squeaks clean under nitrile glove test on random panels.
