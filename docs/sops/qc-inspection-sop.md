# Quality Control Inspection SOP

**Final sign-off before vehicle delivery**

---

## Purpose

Ensure every completed job meets shop standards before customer notification.

## Scope

All installation and detailing services. Lead installer or designated QC inspector signs off.

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Step 1 — Environment
1. Move vehicle to QC zone with adequate cross-lighting
2. Vehicle must be clean enough to evaluate finish (no wash residue on evaluation areas)

### Step 2 — Edge audit
1. Inspect every edge, seam, and termination point
2. Verify post-heat completed per manual standards
3. No lifting, contamination, or incomplete tuck

### Step 3 — Optical inspection
1. View every panel at multiple angles
2. Check for bubbles, fingers, scratches, haze, or debris
3. Document with photos — minimum 4 exterior angles + 2 detail shots

### Step 4 — Sign-off
1. Complete Post-Install QC Checklist
2. Lead installer signs work order
3. Notify CS for delivery scheduling

</div>

## Fail criteria — do not deliver

<div class="callout danger">

Any visible defect, edge lift, contamination, or scope deviation requires rework before delivery. No exceptions for "customer won't notice."

</div>
