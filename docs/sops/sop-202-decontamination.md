# SOP-202 — Decontamination

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 3: Installation Operations**

---

## Purpose

Remove bonded contaminants so film adheres to a chemically clean surface.

## Scope

All PPF, wrap, and TPU installs. Select steps per service manual.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Chemical decon

1. Iron remover on paint — dwell per product label, rinse thoroughly.
2. Tar and adhesive remover on affected areas only.
3. Panel wipe with IPA 70% (or manufacturer-specified prep solution) immediately before install.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Mechanical decon

1. Clay bar or clay mitt with lubricant on paint surfaces receiving film.
2. Glass: steel wool 0000 + glass cleaner on tint jobs only where manual specifies.
3. Final IPA wipe — one direction, fresh towel per section.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Contamination control

1. No silicone-based dressings in install bay before or during job.
2. Installers wash hands; nitrile gloves for final prep.
3. Vehicle cannot leave decon bay through customer waiting area.

**Checkpoint:** Water break test passes on paint (sheet flow, no beading spots) before PPF/wrap.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero adhesion failures traced to prep on QC review</li>
<li>IPA wipe within 30 minutes of first panel install</li>
</ul>
