# SOP-701 — Collecting Payments

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 8: Business Operations**

---

## Purpose

Collect final payment securely and document for accounting.

## Scope

At vehicle pickup or per approved billing terms.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Payment collection

1. Verify balance due against invoice.
2. Accept approved payment methods — card, cash, check, fleet account.
3. Process card present when possible; no card numbers over SMS.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Receipt & close

1. Issue receipt; customer copy and shop copy.
2. CRM payment status → paid; attach transaction ID.
3. Do not release keys until payment clears (except pre-approved accounts).

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Payment recorded before keys released — 100%</li>
<li>Daily cash reconciliation matches POS</li>
</ul>
