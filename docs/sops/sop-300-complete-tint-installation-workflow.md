# SOP-300 — Complete Tint Installation Workflow

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 4: Window Tint**

---

## Purpose

End-to-end procedure for professional window tint installation from bay setup through QC.

## Scope

All automotive window tint services. Follow Window Tint Master Install Manual for film-specific detail.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Pre-install

1. Verify legal VLT limits for customer state and window location.
2. Complete SOP-200 through SOP-204.
3. Protect interior: seat covers, dash mat, door panel tape on switches.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Glass preparation

1. Clean exterior glass for patterning reference if needed.
2. Interior glass: scrub with dedicated glass cleaner, scrape embedded particles with razor (45° angle, wet), final IPA wipe.
3. Roll down windows 1" to clean top edge channel.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Film application

1. Cut pattern (plotter or hand) with 1/16" shrink allowance.
2. Shrink on outside or heat-form per film type; never overheat ceramic.
3. Apply slip solution; position film; squeegee center-out; tack edges last.
4. Heat post-form edges; wipe solution from film and glass.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### QC & cure instructions

1. Inspect for contamination, fingers, and light gaps.
2. Complete SOP-205 and SOP-206.
3. Instruct customer: 3–5 day window cure, no rolling down until verified.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero dust particles visible at 18" in final QC light</li>
<li>Edges uniformly tucked — no gap at defroster lines</li>
</ul>
