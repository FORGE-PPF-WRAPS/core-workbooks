# SOP-105 — Estimate Process

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Produce accurate, professional written estimates that protect margin and set clear scope.

## Scope

All custom quotes — PPF, tint, wrap, TPU, ceramic, detailing packages.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Discovery

1. Confirm vehicle year, make, model, trim, color, and condition.
2. Identify customer goals: coverage level, film brand tier, timeline, budget sensitivity.
3. Inspect vehicle in person when possible — photos are not sufficient for complex wrap or full PPF.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Build the estimate

1. Use service-specific pricing guide — PPF, tint, wrap, coating, or detailing matrix.
2. Line-item: materials, labor, removal, correction add-ons, tax, and total.
3. Note exclusions: existing damage, paint correction requirements, film waste on complex colors.
4. Enter estimate in CRM; generate Service Estimate / Quote PDF.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Present and close

1. Walk customer through scope — what is included and what is not.
2. Valid for 30 days unless material costs change > 5%.
3. Offer deposit collection per SOP-106 to secure schedule slot.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Written estimate on every job over $500</li>
<li>Estimate-to-invoice variance under 10% without signed change order</li>
</ul>
