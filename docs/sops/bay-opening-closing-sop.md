# Daily Bay Opening & Closing SOP

**Start and end of every production day**

---

## Opening procedure

<div class="procedure">

1. Lights on — verify all bay lighting operational
2. Sweep and blow floors; empty trash
3. Wipe work surfaces with approved cleaner
4. Verify slip solution, IPA, and consumables stocked
5. Heat guns, plotters, and tools on shadow board — accounted for
6. Review day's job queue and material pull list
7. Team huddle — 5 minutes: priorities, risks, coverage gaps

</div>

## Closing procedure

<div class="procedure">

1. All vehicles secured — keys in lockbox, bays labeled
2. Tools cleaned and returned to shadow board
3. Film scraps disposed; material logged in inventory
4. Floors swept; contamination control maintained
5. Equipment off (heat guns, plotters, compressors per shop policy)
6. CRM updated — status on every active job
7. Alarm set; doors locked

</div>
