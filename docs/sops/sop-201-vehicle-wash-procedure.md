# SOP-201 — Vehicle Wash Procedure

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 3: Installation Operations**

---

## Purpose

Remove surface dirt safely before decontamination and film application.

## Scope

All vehicles receiving PPF, wrap, TPU, or coating. Performed in designated wash zone.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Pre-rinse

1. Rinse entire vehicle top-down with pressure washer — max 1200 PSI, 12" standoff.
2. Focus wheel wells and lower panels where grit accumulates.
3. Never spray directly at panel gaps or damaged paint.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Contact wash

1. Two-bucket method: clean soap solution + rinse bucket with grit guards.
2. Microfiber wash mitt only — no brushes on paint.
3. Work top-down; straight-line motion; rinse mitt between panels.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Dry & handoff

1. Blow dry door jambs, mirrors, and emblems with filtered air.
2. Microfiber towel dry — no dragging across dry panels.
3. Move to decon bay immediately or cover vehicle if delay exceeds 30 minutes.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero towel scratches from wash process</li>
<li>Vehicle enters decon within 2 hours of wash</li>
</ul>

## Safety notes

<div class="callout warning">

Wet floors — cones and caution signs required in wash bay.

</div>
