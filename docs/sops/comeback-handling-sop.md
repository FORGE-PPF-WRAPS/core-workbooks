# Comeback Handling SOP

**Root cause analysis and service recovery for rework jobs**

---

## Purpose

Resolve quality failures systematically and prevent repeat defects.

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Step 1 — Intake the comeback
1. Listen fully — do not argue or blame customer
2. Photograph reported defect under proper lighting
3. Log in CRM as comeback — link to original job

### Step 2 — Diagnose
1. Lead installer inspects and documents root cause
2. Classify: installer error, material defect, environmental, customer damage, or expectation gap
3. Complete Comeback Analysis section on work order

### Step 3 — Resolve
1. Schedule rework at earliest slot — priority over new jobs when safety/quality at risk
2. Perform rework per original manual procedure — no shortcuts
3. Re-QC with different inspector when possible

### Step 4 — Close loop
1. Customer walkthrough of correction
2. Document lesson learned in team huddle if pattern detected
3. Update competency training if installer-specific

</div>
