# SOP-107 — Vehicle Delivery

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Complete the customer experience with education, documentation, and a flawless handoff.

## Scope

Every completed job before keys return to customer.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Pre-delivery verification

1. Final Inspection (SOP-206) signed off.
2. Vehicle cleaned of install debris; interior vacuumed if foot traffic occurred.
3. Paperwork packet prepared: warranty certificate, aftercare card, invoice.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Customer walkthrough

1. Review completed work in QC lighting — panel by panel.
2. Explain cure times, wash restrictions, and warranty registration (SOP-702).
3. Customer signs Vehicle Delivery Checklist and accepts vehicle.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Post-delivery

1. Collect final payment per SOP-701 if balance due.
2. Schedule follow-up per SOP-704 and review request per SOP-803.
3. CRM status → delivered; attach delivery photos.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero deliveries without signed delivery checklist</li>
<li>Aftercare card provided 100% of film/coating jobs</li>
</ul>
