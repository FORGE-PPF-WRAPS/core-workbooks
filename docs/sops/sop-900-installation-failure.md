# SOP-900 — Installation Failure

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 10: Emergency Procedures**

---

## Purpose

Respond immediately when an installation cannot be completed to standard.

## Scope

Any installer who identifies a non-recoverable defect, substrate failure, or scope impossibility.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Stop work

1. Halt install immediately — do not "push through."
2. Notify lead installer and manager before customer contact.
3. Photograph condition; preserve removed film if applicable.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Assessment

1. Determine root cause: prep failure, film defect, paint failure, scope error.
2. Document on work order with recommended remedy and timeline.
3. If film defect: quarantine roll lot; contact distributor.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Customer communication

1. Manager calls customer same day — honest explanation, revised ETA.
2. No vehicle delivery until remedy complete and SOP-206 passed.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Manager notified within 30 minutes of stop-work decision</li>
<li>Root cause coded in CRM for trend analysis</li>
</ul>

## Safety notes

<div class="callout warning">

Never leave vehicle in unsafe condition — partial installs secured or film removed.

</div>
