# SOP-103 — Vehicle Check-In

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Document vehicle condition, authorize work, and set expectations before any service begins.

## Scope

All customer vehicles — PPF, tint, wrap, TPU, ceramic, detailing. CS and lead tech at drop-off.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Greet and verify

1. Confirm customer name, service scope, and estimated completion date.
2. Verify deposit received if required per SOP-106.
3. Open or update job in CRM / work order system.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Walkaround inspection

1. Inspect all four sides, roof, glass, wheels, and interior (if applicable) under adequate lighting.
2. Document every pre-existing defect on Vehicle Inspection Form.
3. Photograph all panels and all noted damage — timestamp photos.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Authorization

1. Review scope and price with customer.
2. Customer signs Service Agreement and inspection form.
3. Collect keys; tag with job number.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Bay handoff

1. Move vehicle to assigned bay or queue.
2. Notify lead technician via work order.
3. Send customer confirmation text with direct contact info and ETA.

**Checkpoint:** No vehicle enters production without signed inspection form.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>100% vehicles have signed inspection form before work starts</li>
<li>All pre-existing damage photographed and noted</li>
</ul>
