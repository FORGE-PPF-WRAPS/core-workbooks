# SOP-001 — Company Mission & Core Values

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 1: Company Operations**

---

## Purpose

Define why the shop exists and the non-negotiable values that govern every decision, interaction, and installation.

## Scope

All owners, managers, installers, and customer-facing staff. Posted in break room and referenced during onboarding.

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Owner / GM | Maintains mission statement; models values in daily decisions |
| All staff | Know mission and values; escalate conflicts with values to management |

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Mission statement

1. Forge exists to deliver flawless automotive protection and restyling through disciplined craft, honest communication, and repeatable systems.
2. Every vehicle leaving the shop represents our reputation — quality is not optional.
3. We train installers to professional standards because shortcuts create comebacks, not profit.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Core values

1. **Craftsmanship** — Do the work right the first time. Rework is failure, not a second chance.
2. **Integrity** — Document condition honestly. Never sell what the vehicle does not need.
3. **Accountability** — Own mistakes immediately. Fix them before the customer discovers them.
4. **Respect** — Treat every customer vehicle as if it were your own.
5. **Continuous improvement** — Follow SOPs, then improve them through technical bulletins and QC data.

**Checkpoint:** New hires can recite mission and all five values within first week.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Mission and values posted in shop and included in employee handbook</li>
<li>Referenced in pre-shift huddles at least weekly</li>
</ul>
