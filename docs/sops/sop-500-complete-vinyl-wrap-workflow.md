# SOP-500 — Complete Vinyl Wrap Workflow

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 6: Vinyl Wrap**

---

## Purpose

End-to-end procedure for cast vinyl color-change and commercial wrap installs.

## Scope

Full and partial vinyl wraps. Follow Vinyl Wrap Master Install Manual for compound curves and inlays.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Pre-install

1. Complete SOP-200 through SOP-204.
2. Remove or mask hardware per coverage map — photograph before removal.
3. Acclimate film roll to bay temperature minimum 2 hours.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Surface prep

1. Wash and decon per SOP-201 and SOP-202.
2. IPA wipe; ensure zero wax or sealant residue.
3. Degrease door jambs and fuel door if wrapped.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Application

1. Measure and cut with 2" bleed; label pieces by panel.
2. Apply dry or wet per film manufacturer — consistent method per job.
3. Post-heat all recessed areas and edges; use thermometer.
4. Knifeless tape or bladeless cutting at panel gaps where specified.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Reassembly & QC

1. Reinstall hardware with correct torque — no stripped clips.
2. Complete SOP-205 and SOP-206.
3. Customer aftercare: no wash 7 days; hand wash only first 30 days.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>No lifting at edges or mirrors at 30-day follow-up</li>
<li>Color match across panels — no visible shade shift</li>
</ul>
