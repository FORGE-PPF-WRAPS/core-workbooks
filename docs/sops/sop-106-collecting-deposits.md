# SOP-106 — Collecting Deposits

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Secure commitment and cover material costs before ordering film or blocking production time.

## Scope

All jobs requiring deposit per shop policy (typically ≥ $1,000 or custom material orders).


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Deposit amount

1. Standard: 50% deposit to schedule; balance due at pickup unless pre-approved account.
2. Custom film orders: 100% material cost deposit before ordering.
3. State deposit is non-refundable within cancellation window per Refund & Cancellation Policy.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Collection

1. Accept card, cash, or approved digital payment — no personal Venmo/Zelle to staff.
2. Issue Deposit & Payment Receipt immediately.
3. Record payment in CRM and work order; update schedule to "confirmed."

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Refunds & transfers

1. Refunds only per published policy — manager approval required.
2. Deposit may transfer to reschedule within 90 days if customer notifies before cutoff.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Deposit collected before custom material order placed</li>
<li>Receipt issued 100% of the time</li>
</ul>
