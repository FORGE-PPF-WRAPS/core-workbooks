# SOP-100 — Answering the Phone

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Deliver a consistent, professional first impression on every inbound call.

## Scope

Customer service, front desk, and any staff answering the main shop line.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Answer within 3 rings

1. Greeting: "Thank you for calling [Shop Name], this is [Name], how can I help you today?"
2. Speak clearly, smile — tone carries through the phone.
3. If you must place on hold: ask permission, hold under 60 seconds, thank them when returning.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Information capture

1. Capture: name, phone, email, vehicle year/make/model, service interest, timeline.
2. Enter lead in CRM before ending call — even if they are "just asking questions."
3. If unable to quote on the phone, schedule consultation or promise callback within 2 business hours.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Transfer & escalation

1. Technical questions beyond your authority → warm transfer to lead installer or manager with context.
2. Angry caller → remain calm, listen, do not argue. Escalate to manager per SOP-902 if needed.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Answer rate ≥ 90% during business hours</li>
<li>Every call logged in CRM same day</li>
</ul>
