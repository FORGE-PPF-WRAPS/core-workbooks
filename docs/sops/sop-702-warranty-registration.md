# SOP-702 — Warranty Registration

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 8: Business Operations**

---

## Purpose

Register customer warranty coverage with complete documentation for claims support.

## Scope

All jobs including shop workmanship and film manufacturer warranties.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Registration data

1. Customer name, contact, VIN, vehicle details.
2. Film product, lot number, coverage areas, install date, installer name.
3. Attach install photos and signed delivery checklist.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Submission

1. Enter in CRM warranty module.
2. Submit manufacturer registration within 7 days if required by film brand.
3. Issue Warranty Certificate to customer at delivery.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>100% warranty registration before delivery</li>
<li>Film lot traceable on every PPF/TPU job</li>
</ul>
