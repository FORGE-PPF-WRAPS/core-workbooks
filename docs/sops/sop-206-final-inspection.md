# SOP-206 — Final Inspection

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 3: Installation Operations**

---

## Purpose

Manager-level sign-off that the vehicle is customer-ready.

## Scope

Every vehicle before customer notification. Shop manager or senior lead.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Documentation review

1. Work order complete — hours, materials, photos attached.
2. QC checklist signed; any rework noted and resolved.
3. Warranty registration data ready for SOP-702.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Customer-ready audit

1. Repeat edge and optical check at customer viewing height.
2. Interior free of tools, trash, and glove residue.
3. Exterior free of tape marks, solution residue, and fingerprints on glass.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Release

1. Sign final inspection line on work order.
2. Notify CS to schedule delivery (SOP-107).
3. Update CRM status → ready for pickup.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Manager sign-off on 100% of retail deliveries</li>
<li>Delivery photos match QC photos — no post-QC damage</li>
</ul>
