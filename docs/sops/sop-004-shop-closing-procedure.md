# SOP-004 — Shop Closing Procedure

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 1: Company Operations**

---

## Purpose

Secure the facility, protect customer vehicles, and close out production records at end of day.

## Scope

Last person on site or designated closing manager. Every business day.

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Closing lead | Executes closing checklist and secures building |
| Installers | Complete bay cleanup before leaving |

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Production closeout

1. All vehicles secured — keys in lockbox, covers on if overnight in bay.
2. Incomplete jobs documented on work order with photos and next-step notes.
3. Waste film, bottles, and sharps disposed per local regulations.
4. Tools returned to shadow boards; heat guns holstered and powered off.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Administrative closeout

1. CRM updated — job status, ETA changes, and customer texts sent.
2. Cash drawer balanced; deposits prepared per accounting schedule.
3. Tomorrow's appointments confirmed or flagged for follow-up.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Security

1. Exterior doors locked; bay doors down.
2. Alarm armed; verify camera system recording.
3. Final walkthrough — no vehicles running, no open chemicals, no lights left on in bays.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero vehicles left unlocked overnight</li>
<li>100% of in-progress jobs have written status on work order</li>
</ul>
