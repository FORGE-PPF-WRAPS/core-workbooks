# Delivery Walkthrough SOP

**Customer pickup and aftercare education**

---

## Purpose

Complete the customer experience, educate on aftercare, and request reviews.

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Step 1 — Vehicle staging
1. Clean exterior for presentation (quick wipe if needed)
2. Position in delivery area with good lighting

### Step 2 — Walkaround with customer
1. Tour vehicle together — point out completed work and best details
2. Allow customer reaction before talking
3. Address any questions before leaving the vehicle

### Step 3 — Aftercare education
1. Provide **Customer Aftercare Card** for their service type
2. Review wash hold time, cure periods, and what to avoid
3. Explain warranty coverage and registration (if applicable)

### Step 4 — Close
1. Provide warranty certificate if applicable
2. Request review (verbal + text link within 24 hours)
3. Thank customer; confirm follow-up call schedule

</div>
