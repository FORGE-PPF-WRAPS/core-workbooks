# SOP-703 — CRM Updates

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 8: Business Operations**

---

## Purpose

Keep customer and job records current so production and follow-up run without gaps.

## Scope

Every team member who touches a job. Minimum updates at check-in, start, QC, and delivery.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Required touchpoints

1. Lead created → appointment scheduled → vehicle checked in → in production → QC → ready → delivered.
2. Each transition updated same day — no batch updates at week end.
3. Photos uploaded at check-in, post-install QC, and delivery.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Notes standard

1. Notes are factual: what was done, what is pending, who owns next step.
2. Tag team members for handoffs (@lead, @cs).

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>CRM status matches physical vehicle location</li>
<li>Zero "where is this car?" calls from CS to bay</li>
</ul>
