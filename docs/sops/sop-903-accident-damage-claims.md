# SOP-903 — Accident/Damage Claims

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 10: Emergency Procedures**

---

## Purpose

Process insurance and liability claims when shop causes vehicle damage.

## Scope

Management only. Legal counsel for severe incidents.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Immediate actions

1. Secure vehicle; preserve scene; photos from all angles.
2. Complete internal incident report within 24 hours.
3. Do not repair damage without customer authorization documented.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Insurance notification

1. Notify shop insurance carrier per policy requirements.
2. Provide customer with insurance contact and claim number.
3. Cooperate with adjuster; supply inspection photos and work orders.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Closure

1. Track claim to resolution in CRM.
2. Post-incident review: root cause and SOP update if systemic.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Incident report filed within 24 hours</li>
<li>Customer updated every 48 hours until resolved</li>
</ul>

## Safety notes

<div class="callout warning">

Injuries: call 911 first. OSHA reportable incidents documented per state law.

</div>
