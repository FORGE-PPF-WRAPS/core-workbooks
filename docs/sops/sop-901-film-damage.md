# SOP-901 — Film Damage

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 10: Emergency Procedures**

---

## Purpose

Handle damaged film stock, installation errors, and customer vehicle damage during service.

## Scope

Installers and managers when film or vehicle is damaged in bay.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Film stock damage

1. Segregate damaged roll; log lot number and damage type.
2. Reorder if inventory insufficient — update customer ETA.
3. Do not use creased or contaminated film on customer vehicles.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Vehicle damage during service

1. Stop work; photograph damage; notify manager immediately.
2. Do not admit liability — document facts only.
3. Manager contacts insurance per policy; customer notified within 2 hours.
4. Complete Accident/Damage Claims procedure if applicable (SOP-903).

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Damage report filed within 24 hours</li>
<li>Customer notified before they discover damage independently</li>
</ul>
