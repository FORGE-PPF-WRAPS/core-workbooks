# SOP-800 — Before & After Photos

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 9: Marketing**

---

## Purpose

Capture consistent, high-quality documentation for marketing and QC records.

## Scope

All jobs where Photo Release Consent is signed.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Before photos (check-in)

1. Same 8 angles every time: front 3/4 L/R, rear 3/4 L/R, straight front, straight rear.
2. Detail shots of damage, unique features, and areas receiving work.
3. Clean background when possible; no shop clutter in frame.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### After photos (QC)

1. Match before angles exactly — same position, same zoom.
2. QC lighting; vehicle clean.
3. Close-up hero shots of edges, hood lines, and craftsmanship.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### File management

1. Upload to CRM same day; tag vehicle and services.
2. Raw files to marketing folder within 48 hours.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Before/after sets on ≥ 90% of eligible jobs</li>
<li>Photo release on file before marketing use</li>
</ul>
