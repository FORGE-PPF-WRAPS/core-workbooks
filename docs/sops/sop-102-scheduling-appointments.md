# SOP-102 — Scheduling Appointments

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Book production time accurately so bays are neither over-committed nor idle.

## Scope

Customer service and managers scheduling drop-off, install, and pickup.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Capacity check

1. Verify bay availability against job complexity — do not book two full front-end PPF jobs in one bay same day.
2. Block buffer time for QC and unexpected rework (minimum 2 hours per major job).
3. Confirm material on hand or order lead time before confirming date.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Booking

1. Create CRM appointment with: customer, vehicle, scope, estimated labor hours, assigned bay, deposit status.
2. Send confirmation text/email with date, time, address, what to bring, and cancellation policy.
3. For multi-day jobs: book drop-off, estimated completion, and pickup as separate calendar events.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Changes & no-shows

1. Reschedules: offer two alternatives; note reason in CRM.
2. No-show: call within 30 minutes; release bay after 2 hours; apply cancellation policy if applicable.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Schedule accuracy — completed on promised date ≥ 95%</li>
<li>Every appointment linked to work order before vehicle arrives</li>
</ul>
