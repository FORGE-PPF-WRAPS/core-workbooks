# SOP-802 — Video Content

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 9: Marketing**

---

## Purpose

Produce short-form video that demonstrates expertise and drives inquiries.

## Scope

Marketing and senior installers with media training.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Production

1. Plan shot list: hook (3 sec), process, reveal.
2. Stable mount or gimbal; shop audio off or licensed music only.
3. No customer faces or plates without release.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Post-production

1. Brand intro/outro template; logo watermark.
2. Length: 15–60 sec Reels/TikTok; 2–5 min YouTube process videos.
3. Upload native to each platform — no cross-posted watermarks.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Minimum 2 video posts per month</li>
<li>All videos reviewed by manager before publish</li>
</ul>
