# SOP-204 — Tool Preparation

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 3: Installation Operations**

---

## Purpose

Ensure every tool is clean, functional, and appropriate before touching customer vehicles.

## Scope

All installers before starting any job.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Cutting & squeegee tools

1. Fresh blade in knife — change every panel or when dull.
2. Inspect squeegees for nicks; swap if edge damaged.
3. Tack cloth squeegee faces before first panel.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Heat & air

1. Heat gun tip clean; test on scrap film for even heat.
2. Compressor moisture drained; air hose filtered.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Chemicals

1. Verify slip solution mix ratio per film manufacturer.
2. IPA bottle labeled with date mixed; discard after 7 days.
3. No unauthorized adhesives or dressings in bay.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero scratch incidents from dull blades in QC quarter</li>
<li>Tool shadow board 100% accounted for at job end</li>
</ul>
