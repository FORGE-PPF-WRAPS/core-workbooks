# Vehicle Intake SOP

**Standard procedure for every vehicle entering the shop**

---

## Purpose

Document vehicle condition, authorize work, and set customer expectations before any service begins.

## Scope

All customer vehicles — PPF, tint, wrap, ceramic, detailing. Applies to CS staff and lead technicians at drop-off.

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Step 1 — Greet and verify appointment
1. Confirm customer name, service scope, and estimated completion date
2. Verify deposit received if required
3. Open job in CRM / work order system

### Step 2 — Walkaround inspection
1. Inspect all four sides, roof, glass, wheels, and interior (if applicable) under adequate lighting
2. Document every pre-existing defect on **Vehicle Inspection Form**
3. Photograph all panels and all noted damage — timestamp photos

### Step 3 — Authorization
1. Review scope and price with customer
2. Customer signs service agreement and inspection form
3. Collect keys; tag with job number

### Step 4 — Bay handoff
1. Move vehicle to assigned bay or queue
2. Notify lead technician via work order
3. Send customer confirmation text with direct contact info

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>100% of vehicles have signed inspection form before work starts</li>
<li>All pre-existing damage photographed and noted</li>
<li>Customer receives written ETA and contact method</li>
</ul>
