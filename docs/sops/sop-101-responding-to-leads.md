# SOP-101 — Responding to Leads

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Convert inbound inquiries into qualified appointments with consistent follow-up.

## Scope

Web forms, SMS, email, social DMs, and walk-in inquiries.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Speed to lead

1. Respond within 15 minutes during business hours; within 2 hours after hours.
2. Use template: thank them, acknowledge vehicle/service, ask one qualifying question, offer two appointment slots.
3. Log source (Google, Instagram, referral) in CRM for marketing attribution.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Qualification

1. Confirm vehicle details, desired coverage, budget range if appropriate, and timeline.
2. Send pricing guide or package overview PDF when customer requests ballpark — never guess on complex jobs.
3. Disqualify respectfully if vehicle or scope is outside shop capabilities — refer when possible.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Follow-up sequence

1. Day 0: initial response. Day 1: follow-up if no reply. Day 3: value-add (gallery link, review snippet). Day 7: final check-in.
2. Mark lead lost with reason after 7 days of no engagement.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Median first-response time under 30 minutes</li>
<li>Lead-to-appointment conversion tracked monthly</li>
</ul>
