# Glass Preparation SOP

**Window tint glass prep before film application**

---

## Purpose

Remove contamination that causes adhesion failure and visual defects on tinted glass.

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Step 1 — Initial clean
1. Spray glass with slip solution; squeegee loose debris
2. Scrape only with fresh blade if adhesive residue present — never on coated glass without verification

### Step 2 — Scrub
1. Apply glass prep solution with scrub pad (dot matrix areas: light pressure)
2. Pay special attention to dot matrix, gaskets, and top edge

### Step 3 — Squeegee and final wipe
1. Squeegee all solution from glass and gaskets
2. Final IPA or glass prep wipe — edge to center
3. Inspect under light — no streaks, fingerprints, or dust

### Step 4 — Install window
1. Film must be laid within contamination-controlled timeframe
2. If delay exceeds 15 minutes — re-prep affected area
