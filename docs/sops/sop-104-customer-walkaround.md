# SOP-104 — Customer Walkaround

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 2: Customer Experience**

---

## Purpose

Build trust by reviewing documented condition with the customer before keys change hands.

## Scope

Performed at check-in and again at delivery. CS or lead tech conducts.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Check-in walkaround

1. Walk vehicle with customer clockwise — front, passenger, rear, driver.
2. Point out each defect already noted; ask "anything we missed?"
3. Explain what areas will be worked on and what will not be touched.
4. Customer initials inspection form at completion.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Delivery walkaround

1. Review completed work panel by panel in QC lighting.
2. Demonstrate edges, seams, and care instructions.
3. Customer signs delivery checklist confirming acceptance.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero "you scratched my car" disputes without pre-existing photo documentation</li>
<li>Walkaround completed on 100% of retail jobs</li>
</ul>
