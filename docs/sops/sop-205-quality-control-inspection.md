# SOP-205 — Quality Control Inspection

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 3: Installation Operations**

---

## Purpose

Verify installation meets shop standards before vehicle moves to final inspection.

## Scope

All installation services. Performed by lead installer or QC-designated tech — not the sole installer on complex jobs.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Environment

1. Move vehicle to QC zone with adequate cross-lighting.
2. Vehicle clean enough to evaluate finish — no wash residue on evaluation areas.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Edge audit

1. Inspect every edge, seam, and termination point.
2. Verify post-heat completed per Master Installer Manual standards.
3. No lifting, contamination, or incomplete tuck.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Optical inspection

1. View every panel at multiple angles.
2. Check for bubbles, fingers, scratches, haze, or debris.
3. Document with photos — minimum 4 exterior angles + 2 detail shots.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Sign-off

1. Complete Post-Install QC Checklist.
2. Lead installer signs work order.
3. Fail → rework before advancing to SOP-206.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Post-install checklist on 100% of jobs</li>
<li>Comeback rate under shop target (track monthly)</li>
</ul>

## Safety notes

<div class="callout warning">

Do not deliver with any visible defect — see fail criteria in Quality Standards Manual.

</div>
