# SOP-803 — Google Review Process

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 9: Marketing**

---

## Purpose

Grow Google reputation through ethical review generation and professional responses.

## Scope

Customer service and management.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Generation

1. Follow SOP-705 for satisfied customers.
2. QR code at delivery desk links to Google review page.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Response protocol

1. Respond to all reviews within 48 hours.
2. Positive: thank customer, mention team, invite return.
3. Negative: apologize, take offline, provide manager contact — never argue publicly.
4. Escalate 1–2 star reviews to SOP-902 immediately.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Average rating maintained ≥ 4.7</li>
<li>100% review response rate</li>
</ul>
