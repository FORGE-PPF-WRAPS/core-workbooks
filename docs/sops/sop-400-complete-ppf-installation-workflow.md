# SOP-400 — Complete PPF Installation Workflow

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 5: Paint Protection Film**

---

## Purpose

End-to-end procedure for paint protection film from surface prep through edge sealing.

## Scope

All PPF services — partial, full front, track, and full body. Follow PPF Master Install Manual for panel specifics.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Pre-install

1. Complete SOP-200 through SOP-204.
2. Confirm paint correction completed if required and approved.
3. Pull patterns; verify kit completeness against coverage map.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Surface prep

1. SOP-201 wash → SOP-202 decon on all panels receiving film.
2. Final IPA wipe panel-by-panel immediately before each piece.
3. Never apply dressing before or during install.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Film application

1. Spray slip solution on panel and adhesive side of film.
2. Position; anchor hinge or center tack per panel geometry.
3. Squeegee with overlapping strokes; evacuate all solution.
4. Wrap edges; post-heat 90–120°C per film spec using IR thermometer.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Complex areas

1. Bumpers and mirrors: relief cuts only per manual — no freehand slashing.
2. Sensors and ADAS: follow manual clearance zones; recalibrate if disturbed.
3. Seams: overlap 3mm minimum where double-layer required.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### QC

1. Complete SOP-205 and SOP-206.
2. Document install with photos for warranty file.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>No visible fingers, contamination, or edge lift at delivery</li>
<li>Post-heat logged on work order for full-front and above</li>
</ul>
