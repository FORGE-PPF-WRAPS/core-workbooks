# SOP-704 — Customer Follow-Up

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 8: Business Operations**

---

## Purpose

Proactive post-service contact to catch issues early and build loyalty.

## Scope

Customer service. All retail jobs.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Follow-up schedule

1. Day 1: text — "How does everything look? Any questions on care?"
2. Day 7: email — aftercare reminder and direct contact for concerns.
3. Day 30: check-in on cure completion (tint windows, coating, wrap wash restrictions).

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Issue handling

1. Any concern → schedule inspection within 48 hours.
2. Document in CRM; escalate to SOP-902 if complaint.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Follow-up completed on 100% of retail jobs</li>
<li>Issues identified before Google review when possible</li>
</ul>
