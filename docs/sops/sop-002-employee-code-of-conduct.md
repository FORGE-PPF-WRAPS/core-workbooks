# SOP-002 — Employee Code of Conduct

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 1: Company Operations**

---

## Purpose

Establish behavioral standards for professionalism, safety, and customer trust.

## Scope

All employees, contractors, and trainees while on premises or representing the shop.

## Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Owner / GM | Enforces policy; documents violations |
| Lead installer | Models conduct on the floor; reports issues |
| All staff | Comply with dress, language, phone, and social media standards |

## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Professional standards

1. Arrive on time, in clean branded or neutral work attire. No offensive graphics.
2. No profanity, arguments, or personal phone use in customer areas.
3. No food or open drinks in installation bays.
4. Customer vehicles: no smoking, no unauthorized test drives, no personal items left inside.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Confidentiality & social media

1. Do not post customer vehicles, pricing disputes, or internal drama on personal social accounts without written release.
2. Do not discuss one customer's job with another customer.
3. Shop marketing content follows SOP-800 through SOP-803 only.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Substance & safety

1. Zero tolerance for alcohol or illegal substances on premises or while operating shop vehicles.
2. Report injuries, near-misses, and equipment damage immediately to management.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Zero unresolved conduct complaints on customer record</li>
<li>Annual acknowledgment signed by every team member</li>
</ul>

## Safety notes

<div class="callout warning">

Violations involving safety or customer property may result in immediate suspension pending review.

</div>
