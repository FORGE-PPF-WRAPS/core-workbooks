# SOP-801 — Social Media Posting

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 9: Marketing**

---

## Purpose

Publish on-brand content that showcases work without exposing customer privacy.

## Scope

Marketing manager or authorized staff only.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Content approval

1. Verify photo release signed.
2. Apply brand filters and templates from Marketing Resources Kit.
3. Caption: service performed, film brand (if allowed), shop tagline, CTA.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Publishing cadence

1. Minimum 3 posts per week across primary platforms.
2. Rotate: finished work, process reels, education, team.
3. Respond to comments and DMs within 4 business hours — route leads to SOP-101.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>100% posts use approved brand assets</li>
<li>Zero customer plates visible without blur</li>
</ul>
