# SOP-902 — Customer Complaint

**Forge Standard Operating Procedures v1.0**

**Section 10: Emergency Procedures**

---

## Purpose

Resolve customer dissatisfaction fairly, quickly, and with full documentation.

## Scope

Any staff receiving a complaint. Manager owns resolution.


## Procedure

<div class="procedure">

### Intake

1. Listen without interrupting; take notes.
2. Apologize for their experience — not necessarily admitting fault.
3. Schedule inspection within 48 hours; offer loaner options if available.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Investigation

1. Review check-in photos, QC records, and installer notes.
2. Inspect vehicle with customer present when possible.
3. Classify: legitimate defect, expectation mismatch, unrelated pre-existing.

</div>

<div class="procedure">

### Resolution

1. Remedy per warranty policy — rework, partial refund, or goodwill gesture.
2. Manager approves any concession over $250.
3. Document resolution in CRM; customer signs if settlement involves release.

</div>

## Quality standards

<ul class="checklist">
<li>First response within 4 business hours</li>
<li>Resolution plan within 48 hours of inspection</li>
</ul>
