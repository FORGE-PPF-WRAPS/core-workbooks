## 1. Engagement

Company engages Contractor to perform the services described in **Exhibit A** (Scope of Services). Contractor accepts such engagement and agrees to perform services as an independent contractor, not as an employee, partner, or agent of Company.

---

## 2. Independent contractor status

Contractor represents and agrees that:

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Contractor is free to control the manner and means of performing services, subject to Company quality standards</li>
<li>Contractor is responsible for all taxes, withholdings, and insurance applicable to Contractor</li>
<li>Contractor will not receive employee benefits from Company</li>
<li>Contractor may perform services for other clients, provided no conflict with Section 8 (Confidentiality)</li>
<li>Contractor will supply own tools unless otherwise agreed in writing</li>
</ul>
