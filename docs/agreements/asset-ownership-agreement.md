# Asset Ownership Agreement

**Ownership and use of tools, equipment, and materials**

---

## Parties

Effective **[EFFECTIVE DATE]**:

**Company:** [COMPANY LEGAL NAME] ("Company")  
**Party B:** ☐ Employee ☐ Contractor ☐ Partner: [NAME] ("User")

This Asset Ownership Agreement ("Agreement") defines ownership and permitted use of assets in connection with services performed for or with Company.

---

## 1. Company-owned assets

The following categories remain **exclusive property of Company** at all times:

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Tools, heat guns, squeegees, plotters, and installation equipment provided by Company</li>
<li>Film, vinyl, chemicals, and consumables purchased by Company</li>
<li>Computers, software licenses, pattern databases, and plotter files</li>
<li>Training materials, SOPs, workbooks, and digital curricula</li>
<li>Branded signage, uniforms, and marketing materials</li>
<li>Vehicles, bays, and facility fixtures</li>
</ul>

User receives **limited right to use** Company assets solely for authorized work during engagement. No ownership interest transfers.

---

## 2. User-supplied assets

Tools or equipment brought by User ("User Assets") remain User property unless:

☐ Purchased by Company under reimbursement agreement (then Company owns)  
☐ Explicitly transferred in writing (list in Exhibit A)

Company is not responsible for loss or damage to User Assets except Company negligence.

---

## 3. Custody & return

Upon request or termination of relationship, User shall immediately return all Company assets in good working order, ordinary wear excepted. User authorizes Company to withhold final payment for unreturned or damaged assets, subject to applicable law.

**Return checklist:**

<ul class="checklist">
<li>Keys / access cards</li>
<li>Tools & equipment (itemized)</li>
<li>Film inventory</li>
<li>Laptops / tablets</li>
<li>Software media / login credentials disabled</li>
<li>Branded materials</li>
</ul>

---

## 4. Loss & damage

User shall report loss, theft, or damage of Company assets within **24 hours**. User may be responsible for replacement cost if loss results from User negligence or willful misconduct.

---

## 5. Intellectual property assets

Digital assets — including customer lists, templates, pricing sheets, and training files — are Company confidential property. User shall not copy, retain, or use after relationship ends.

---

## 6. Purchase option (optional)

Company may offer User right of first refusal to purchase designated equipment upon separation at fair market value. Terms in separate written agreement.

---

**[STATE]** law applies.
