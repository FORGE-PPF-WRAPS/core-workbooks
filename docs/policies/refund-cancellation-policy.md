## Deposits

<ul class="checklist">

## Cancellations

| Timing | Policy |
|--------|--------|
| 7+ days before appointment | Full deposit refund or transfer |
| 3–6 days before | 50% deposit retained |
| Less than 48 hours | Deposit forfeited |
| After work started | No refund; customer pays for work completed + materials used |

## Refunds on completed work

Refunds are not issued for completed work except where required by law or at shop management discretion for documented service failure not resolved by rework.
