# Chapter 87 — Communication Script Library

Phone and in-person scripts for customer-facing operations.

### Inbound Phone — New Lead
<div class="script-block">"Thank you for calling [Shop Name]. This is [Name]. How can I help protect your vehicle today?"</div>
### Price Inquiry — Hold Frame
<div class="script-block">"I can give you an accurate quote after I know your vehicle and the coverage you want. May I get your year, make, and model?"</div>
### Complaint — Listen First
<div class="script-block">"I am sorry you are experiencing this. I want to fix it. Tell me exactly what you are seeing and when you first noticed it."</div>
### Delivery — Set Expectations
<div class="script-block">"Here is your aftercare sheet. No wash for 48 hours. Edges are curing — avoid picking at any small bubbles unless we discussed them."</div>
### Upsell — Consultative
<div class="script-block">"Based on how you use this vehicle, many owners in your situation add rocker protection. Want me to show you why on your car?"</div>
