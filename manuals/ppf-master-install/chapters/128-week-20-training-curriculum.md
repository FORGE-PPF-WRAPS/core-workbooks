# Chapter 128 — Week 20 Training Curriculum


**Week 20** training focus for PPF Installation team. Lead installer runs 30-minute Monday briefing; technician completes practical by Friday.


## Learning Objectives


<ul class="checklist">
<li>Define week 20 quality standard in your own words</li>
<li>Demonstrate one new technique from this week on practice or live job</li>
<li>Complete QC checklist without lead prompting</li>
<li>Document one lesson learned in shop log</li>
</ul>


## Classroom Module (30 min)


<div class="procedure">

### Week 20 — Classroom Session

1. Review previous week comeback data — what pattern emerged?
2. Present this week topic with photos or sample panels
3. Open Q&A — no question is too basic
4. Assign practical exercise and due date
5. Post one-page summary in shop channel

</div>
