# Chapter 26 — Case Studies & Team Discussion

Real-world scenario case studies for PPF Installation team training. Discuss in weekly shop meetings.

### Case Study 1

**Scenario:** A customer returns 3 days after service reporting a quality concern on a high-visibility area.

#### Discussion Questions

<ul class="checklist">
<li>What intake documentation protects the shop?</li>
<li>What is the immediate customer communication script?</li>
<li>Does this require redo, repair, or education?</li>
<li>What systemic change prevents recurrence?</li>
</ul>

<div class="callout tip">

**Resolution principle:** Fix the customer first. Fix the process second. Never argue about coverage before listening.

</div>
