# Chapter 23 — Climate & Environmental Adaptation

Environmental conditions directly affect PPF Installation outcomes. This chapter defines adaptation standards — not optional variations.

### Hot / Arid

Accelerated adhesive cure; manage slip drying speed. Heat post-heat carefully — surface temps exceed 140°F in sun.

<div class="callout tip">

**Hot / Arid standard:** Log ambient temperature and humidity on every job card. Comeback patterns frequently trace to ignored environmental variables.

</div>

### Hot / Humid

Extended cure before edge trimming. Dehumidify bay when possible.

<div class="callout tip">

**Hot / Humid standard:** Log ambient temperature and humidity on every job card. Comeback patterns frequently trace to ignored environmental variables.

</div>

### Cold / Northern

Film acclimation 24hr minimum. Extend wash hold on customer aftercare.

<div class="callout tip">

**Cold / Northern standard:** Log ambient temperature and humidity on every job card. Comeback patterns frequently trace to ignored environmental variables.

</div>

### Coastal / Salt

Emphasize corrosion-prone zones: rockers, behind wheels, lower doors.

<div class="callout tip">

</div>

### High Altitude UV

Recommend premium UV-stable film tiers. Annual inspection upsell.

</div>
