# Chapter 46 — Technician Competency Standards

Technician competency verification standards for Paint Correction & Detailing. These are shop certification gates — not classroom exercises. Each standard must be demonstrated on live or controlled work before independent authorization.

### Competency Standard 1

<div class="procedure">

### Standard 1 — Verification Protocol

1. Technician reviews applicable SOP section before demonstration
2. Lead installer observes full execution without intervention
3. All safety and quality checkpoints met per rubric below
4. Documentation completed on competency record
5. Authorization signed by lead installer and shop manager
