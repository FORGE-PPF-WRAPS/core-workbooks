# Chapter 47 — Field Scenario Analysis

Field scenario analysis for Paint Correction & Detailing operations. Use in lead installer meetings, comeback reviews, and SOP updates.

### Scenario 1 — Operational Analysis

**Situation:** A quality concern is reported on a high-visibility area 2 days after service completion.

#### Required Analysis

<ul class="checklist">
<li>What intake documentation establishes baseline condition?</li>
<li>What work order scope governs coverage expectations?</li>
<li>Is this defect, damage, or customer misuse?</li>
<li>What corrective action is authorized under warranty policy?</li>
<li>What SOP change prevents recurrence?</li>
</ul>

<div class="callout warning">

**Operational principle:** Resolve the customer first. Correct the process second. Never debate coverage before completing intake review.

</div>
