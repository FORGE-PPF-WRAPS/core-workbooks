# Chapter 134 — Technical Bulletin — Monthly KPI review


Mandatory shop bulletin for Paint Correction & Detailing: **Monthly KPI review**. Effective immediately for all jobs.


## Purpose


Establish non-negotiable standards for monthly kpi review across all Paint Correction & Detailing operations.


<div class="procedure">

### Monthly KPI review — Standard Workflow

1. Review bulletin before shift start
2. Verify tools and documentation available
3. Apply on every applicable job without exception
4. Log compliance on job card
5. Report deviations to lead installer same day

</div>


## Pass / Fail Criteria


| Checkpoint | Pass | Fail Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Documentation | Complete before keys returned | Hold delivery |
| Technique | Matches SOP | Rework before release |
| Safety | Zero violations | Stop work |


<div class="callout warning">

**Mandatory:** Monthly KPI review applies to retail, fleet, warranty rework, and mobile jobs without exception.

</div>
