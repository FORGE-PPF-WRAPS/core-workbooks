## Appendix A — Coating Product Comparison Reference

| Attribute | Entry Grade | Mid Grade | Professional Grade | Elite Grade |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| SiO₂ Content | 10–30% | 40–60% | 60–80% | 80–92% |

## Appendix B — Environmental Condition Decision Chart

| Temperature | Humidity | Recommendation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Under 10 °C | Any | Do not apply — cure will not initiate properly; reschedule |
| 10–15 °C | Under 50% | Proceed with caution; extend working time budget; use IR cure lamp |
| 15–25 °C | 40–65% | Optimal conditions — proceed with standard protocol |
| 25–30 °C | 50–70% | Reduce working section size; monitor flash time actively |
| 30+ °C | Any | Do not apply outdoors or in uncontrolled bay; reschedule |
| Any temperature | Above 70% RH | Do not apply — flash time too unpredictable; high-spot risk elevated |

## Appendix C — Post-Service Customer Aftercare Guide Template

<div class="callout tip">

**YOUR COATING AFTERCARE GUIDE**

**First 7 days:** No washing; no water contact if possible; avoid parking under trees; do not apply wax or protectant.

**Days 7–30:** Hand wash only with pH-neutral soap; avoid automatic car washes; no wax or polish; remove bird droppings, sap, and tar within 24 hours.

**After 30 days:** Continue hand washing with pH-neutral products. Schedule your annual maintenance check at 12 months.

**Never use:** Abrasive polish, "cleaner wax" products, brush car washes, or pH-aggressive cleaners.
